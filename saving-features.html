Saving

Automatically save at your speed

With SmartFin you don't have to keep reminders to remember to invest each month. You can simply create rules and automations that will make sure a portion of earnings end up in your desired investments. If things change you can simply increase or decrease the amount or cadence at any time.

Saving

Keep your saving goals in mind

Saving can feel really abstract at times, that why we centre your savings around the things you are actually saving for. Invest in the future 'you' - by using saving pots to remind yourself what the end goal is.

Saving

Save directly from your pay check

We work with your employer to reserve and direct a portion of your salary straight into your savings account. This a great way to 'pay yourself first' and ensure that you always make a contribution to your savings, the same way you pay tax and pension contributions.
